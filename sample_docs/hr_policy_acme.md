# Acme Corp — Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

## Annual Leave Policy

Full-time employees accrue 22 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Leave accrues monthly at a rate of 1.83 days. Unused leave may be carried over
to the next year up to a maximum of 5 days; any excess is forfeited on March 31.
Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 10 business days
in advance for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements require VP-level approval and are reviewed every
6 months. Remote employees must be reachable during core hours, defined as
10:00–16:00 in the Istanbul (UTC+3) time zone. The company provides a one-time
home office stipend of $500 for eligible remote employees.

## Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of being incurred, with
receipts attached. Expenses above $200 require pre-approval from the budget
owner. Per-diem for domestic business travel is $60 per day; international
travel per-diem is $95 per day. Mileage is reimbursed at $0.45 per kilometer
for personal vehicle use on approved business trips.

## Probation and Performance Reviews

New hires complete a 3-month probation period, extendable once by 3 months.
Performance reviews are held twice a year, in June and December. Promotion
cycles align with the December review. Employees on a performance improvement
plan (PIP) are reviewed monthly for the duration of the plan, which lasts
between 60 and 90 days.

## Information Security Basics

All employees must enable multi-factor authentication on company accounts.
Company data may not be stored on personal devices without an approved MDM
profile. Lost or stolen devices must be reported to security@acme.example
within 24 hours. Sharing credentials is grounds for disciplinary action.
