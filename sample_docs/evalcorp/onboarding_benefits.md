# EvalCorp — Onboarding & Benefits Guide

## Probation and Reviews

New hires complete a 4-month probation period with a mid-point check-in at
week 8. Performance reviews run twice a year, in May and November; promotion
decisions are made only in the November cycle. Probation may be extended once,
by at most 2 months.

## Health and Insurance

Private health insurance starts on day one and covers spouses and children at
no extra cost. Dental coverage begins after probation. The company matches
voluntary pension (BES) contributions up to 4% of gross salary.

## Meal and Commute

Employees receive a meal card loaded with 350 TRY per working day. A commute
allowance of 2500 TRY per month is paid to employees who attend the office at
least 8 days that month; it is not paid during fully remote months.

## Learning Budget

Every employee has an annual learning budget of 30000 TRY for courses, books,
conferences and certifications, approved by their manager. Unused budget does
not roll over. One paid conference day per quarter is granted in addition to
annual leave.

## Referral Program

Successful referrals earn a 40000 TRY gross bonus, paid after the referred
employee completes probation. Referrals for hard-to-fill roles listed by
Talent Acquisition earn double.
