# [ARCHIVED — 2023, SUPERSEDED] EvalCorp Leave Policy (old version)

> This document is retained for audit purposes only. It was superseded in 2024.

## Annual Leave (2023 rules)

Full-time employees accrued 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Up to 3 unused days could be carried into the next year, expiring on June 30.
Leave requests longer than 5 consecutive days required 10 business days of
advance notice by email to the HR mailbox.

## Sick Leave (2023 rules)

Employees received 7 paid sick days per year. Absences of 2 or more consecutive
days required a doctor's note within 72 hours.

## Parental Leave (2023 rules)

Primary caregivers received 12 weeks of paid parental leave; secondary
caregivers received 2 weeks. Leave had to be taken as a single continuous block
and HR notified 6 weeks ahead.

## Special Leave (2023 rules)

Bereavement leave was 3 days for immediate family. Marriage granted 2 days of
paid leave. Unpaid leave was capped at 1 month after 3 years of tenure.
