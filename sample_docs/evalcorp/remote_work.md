# EvalCorp — Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

## Hybrid Schedule

Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week; Tuesdays and Thursdays are
mandatory office days for all teams. Fully remote arrangements require director
approval and are re-evaluated every quarter.

## Core Hours and Availability

Remote employees must be online and reachable during core hours, 09:30–15:30
Istanbul time (UTC+3). Calendar status must reflect actual availability, and
camera-on is required for customer-facing calls.

## Equipment and Stipend

The company provides a laptop and one external monitor for home use. Eligible
remote employees receive a one-time home-office stipend of 9000 TRY and a
monthly internet allowance of 400 TRY, paid with payroll.

## Remote Work Abroad

Working from outside Turkey is allowed for at most 30 calendar days per year,
only from countries on the approved list maintained by Legal, and requires
security team sign-off 10 business days in advance. Tax residency implications
are the employee's responsibility beyond 30 days.

## Workspace Requirements

Remote workspaces must have a lockable room or screen-privacy arrangement when
handling customer data. Public Wi-Fi may be used only through the corporate VPN.
