# EvalCorp — Leave Policy

## Annual Leave

Full-time employees accrue 24 days of paid annual leave per calendar year,
accruing at 2 days per month. Up to 6 unused days may be carried into the next
year; carried days expire on April 30. Leave requests longer than 4 consecutive
days require 15 business days of advance notice through the HR portal.

## Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year. Absences of 3 or more consecutive
days require a doctor's note submitted within 48 hours of returning. Unused sick
days do not carry over and are not paid out.

## Parental Leave

Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave; secondary
caregivers receive 4 weeks. Parental leave may be split into at most 2 blocks
within the first 12 months after birth or adoption. Employees must notify HR at
least 8 weeks before the intended start date.

## Bereavement and Special Leave

Employees are entitled to 5 days of paid bereavement leave for immediate family
and 1 day for extended family. Marriage grants 3 days of paid leave. Jury duty
is paid in full for its duration upon presentation of the court summons.

## Unpaid Leave

After 2 years of tenure, employees may request up to 3 months of unpaid leave
once every 5 years, subject to VP approval and a 60-day notice period.
