# [ARCHIVED — 2023, SUPERSEDED] EvalCorp Travel & Expense Policy (old version)

> This document is retained for audit purposes only. It was superseded in 2024.

## Per-Diem Rates (2023 rules)

Domestic per-diem was 950 TRY per day. International per-diem was 70 EUR per
day for Europe and 85 USD per day elsewhere. Receipts were required for any
claim above 500 TRY.

## Transportation (2023 rules)

Economy class was mandatory on all flights regardless of duration. Mileage for
personal vehicles was reimbursed at 8 TRY per kilometer. Ride-shares required
line-manager pre-approval.

## Accommodation (2023 rules)

Hotel bookings were capped at 4000 TRY per night domestically and 120 EUR
internationally. No allowance existed for staying with family or friends.

## Submission (2023 rules)

Expense reports were due within 45 days of trip completion. Corporate card
statements were reconciled quarterly.
