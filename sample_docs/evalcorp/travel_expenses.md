# EvalCorp — Travel & Expense Policy

## Booking and Approval

All business travel must be booked through the corporate travel portal at least
7 days in advance for domestic trips and 14 days for international trips. Trips
estimated above 25000 TRY require pre-approval from the budget owner and a VP.

## Per-Diem Rates

Domestic per-diem is 1800 TRY per day. International per-diem is 90 EUR per day
for Europe and 110 USD per day for other regions. Per-diem covers meals and
incidentals; no receipts are required for per-diem claims.

## Transportation

Economy class is standard for all flights under 6 hours; premium economy is
allowed for flights over 6 hours. Mileage for personal vehicle use on approved
trips is reimbursed at 14 TRY per kilometer. Taxis and ride-shares are
reimbursable with receipts when public transit is impractical.

## Accommodation

Hotel bookings are capped at 7500 TRY per night domestically and 180 EUR per
night internationally. Stays with family or friends instead of a hotel earn a
flat 600 TRY per night allowance.

## Expense Submission

Expense reports are due within 21 days of trip completion with itemized
receipts attached. Items above 3000 TRY submitted without a receipt are not
reimbursed. Corporate card holders must reconcile statements monthly by the
5th business day.
