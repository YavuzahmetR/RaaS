# EvalCorp — Information Security Policy

## Authentication

Multi-factor authentication is mandatory on all company accounts. Passwords
must be at least 16 characters, generated and stored in the approved password
manager. Password rotation is required every 12 months or immediately after a
suspected compromise. Shared credentials are prohibited and are a disciplinary
offense.

## Devices

Company data may only be accessed from devices enrolled in MDM. Personal
devices require an approved MDM work profile before accessing email or
documents. Disk encryption and automatic screen lock (max 5 minutes) are
enforced by policy on all endpoints.

## Incident Reporting

Suspected security incidents must be reported to the SOC within 30 minutes of
discovery via the #sec-incident channel or the 24/7 hotline. Lost or stolen
devices must be reported within 4 hours. Employees must not attempt to
investigate or remediate incidents themselves.

## Data Handling

Customer data is classified Confidential by default and may not be exported to
personal storage or third-party AI tools without a signed DPA. Production data
may never be copied to development environments; use the approved anonymized
datasets instead.

## Access Reviews

Manager-led access reviews run quarterly. Access unused for 90 days is revoked
automatically. Privileged (admin) access requires a ticket, expires after 12
hours, and is logged end to end.
